## Key Discussion Points

### Lead generation goals (locked)

- **175–200 meetings per month** is the target. Sales team capacity is currently underutilized (close rate ~20%, ~3 calls/day vs ~12/day capacity).
- Subscription / retainer over project-based work for predictability. Project work today is ~50% of revenue and acts as the entry point ($800–$1,100) before clients land-and-expand to $2,500+/mo subscriptions.

### Service packages (locked)

- **10 hrs/wk** — entry tier, smaller volume
- **20 hrs/wk** — mid tier
- **40 hrs/wk** — full pod
- Video volume scales 2–20 videos/week depending on tier and complexity

### Client problem types Viral addresses (David's framing)

1. **Editing bottleneck** — too much footage, not enough creative velocity
2. **Content uncertainty** — buyer doesn't know what content to make (problem Viral expects to grow as AI is integrated into more marketing teams)
3. **Strained in-house marketing teams** — small teams outsource video for scalability

### Vertical prioritization (locked)

| Vertical | Priority | Why |
|----------|----------|-----|
| Healthcare / Pharma | **PRIORITY 1** | Largest deal sizes, longest LTV, strongest case study library (J&J, McKesson, Janssen, Cortechs.ai, Inbody) |
| Marketing / PR Agencies | **PRIORITY 2** | Fastest sales cycle. Multiplier effect (one agency = access to 5–500 of their clients) |
| Fintech | Tertiary | Sticky once landed (regulation, complexity). Specialized; harder to segment initially |
| Law Firms | **DEPRIORITIZED** | US legal-marketing compliance risk; Zach flagged reportability concerns |
| Ecommerce/DTC, SaaS/B2B, Pro Services | Reserved for second wave | Validate first vertical before spreading |

### Decision-maker roles (by company size)

**Healthcare $1M+:**
- Target: VP Marketing, Senior Director of Brand, Head of Content, Senior Business Director
- Avoid CEOs at large companies (target sub-divisions instead)
- CEO/Founder only if company is below $5M revenue
- NEVER finance or accounting

**Marketing agencies $5M–$50M (30–150 employees):**
- Target: Founder, CEO, Head of Production, Marketing Managers, CMOs, Brand/Social/Video leads (those with corporate cards)
- Do NOT target executive assistants or brand ambassadors
